About Our Project

Our stock price prediction tool uses advanced LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) networks to forecast the price of a stock for the 8th day based on the previous 7 days' historical data. By leveraging the power of deep learning, we provide accurate predictions with a reliability score based on the accuracy of our model's performance. This trust factor helps users assess the quality of the prediction, ensuring transparency and informed decision-making. Always remember that stock markets are inherently volatile, and predictions should be used as a supplementary tool rather than a sole basis for financial decisions.
